Welcome to The Fitchen! Behind the recipes, the food, and the fitness we are a married couple living in beautiful Colorado Springs. Jordan writes the posts and takes the pictures (hi!), and Clark handles the tech and design aspects of the blog. The recipe development, grocery shopping, prep, cooking, and cleanup are all a joint effort. We have a silly, loveable dog named Scout and we split our time between cooking and camping in the great outdoors.
